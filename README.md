# 🚀 Work2Business

**Work2Business** is a modern career transformation platform that helps users transition from employment to entrepreneurship. It enables users to build professional profiles, track employment history, and generate AI-powered business plans based on their skills, experience, and background.

The platform is designed to empower individuals to turn their career journey into real business opportunities.

---

## ✨ Features

- 👤 **User Profile System**
  - Create and manage personal profiles
  - Store professional information and skills

- 💼 **Employment History Tracking**
  - Add and manage job experiences
  - Build a structured career timeline

- 🤖 **AI Business Plan Generator**
  - Generate business ideas based on user profile
  - Create structured business plans:
    - Executive Summary
    - Market Analysis
    - Business Model
    - Marketing Strategy
    - Financial Plan
    - Risk Analysis

- 📁 **Business Plan Management**
  - Save multiple generated plans
  - View detailed plan breakdowns
  - Search and filter plans
  - Delete plans when needed

- 🔐 **Authentication System**
  - Secure login and registration
  - Protected routes
  - Persistent user sessions

- 📱 **Responsive UI**
  - Modern SaaS-style interface
  - Mobile-friendly design
  - Clean dashboard layout

---

## 🎯 Platform Vision

Work2Business aims to bridge the gap between employment and entrepreneurship by:
- Helping users understand their career potential
- Transforming experience into business ideas
- Providing structured AI-driven business guidance
- Supporting users in building financial independence
